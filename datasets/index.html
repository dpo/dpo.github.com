Data Sets

Linear Systems from Interior Methods

A flexible collection of linear systems arising from interior-point methods for quadratic optimization in MatrixMarket format. The systems are supplied in the form of their blocks, which can be rearranged, along with accompanying right-hand sides.

Symmetric Quasi-Definite Systems from Optimization

A collection of symmetric and quasi-definite linear systems from optimization in MatrixMarket format.
